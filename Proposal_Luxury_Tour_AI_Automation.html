📋 Phase-by-Phase Implementation Plan

PHASE 1 CRM Foundation, Lead Capture & Sales Funnel Weeks 1–3

What gets built:

  • GoHighLevel account fully configured: pipeline stages, custom fields, team users, sub-accounts
  • Lead capture integrations: website forms, Instagram/Facebook lead ads, TikTok lead forms — all flow into GHL automatically
  • AI lead qualification agent: when a new lead comes in, the AI starts a conversation (SMS + email), asks qualifying questions (preferred car, date, group size, budget) and scores the lead
  • Lost-lead re-engagement: if a lead views the booking page but doesn't complete it, AI triggers a follow-up sequence and asks "what stopped you?" — answers get tagged (price, timing, questions, etc.) so you can track objections by volume
  • Automated nurture sequences: 7-touch email + SMS sequences for cold leads, warm leads, and post-inquiry leads — all personalized with their vehicle interest
  • Booking funnel: GHL landing page or Stripe Payment Link embedded in booking confirmation flow
Deliverables: Live GHL CRM · Lead capture from all channels · AI follow-up agent · Objection tracking · Booking funnel with payment
PHASE 2 Digital Waiver, Document Collection & Onboarding Weeks 4–6

What gets built:

  • Digital waiver created from your current paper agreement — legally formatted, e-signature enabled via PandaDoc/DocuSign
  • Automated waiver dispatch: triggers immediately after payment confirmation, sent via email + SMS with a personalized link
  • Document upload portal: custom-built secure page (or Typeform + AWS S3 integration) that collects:
    • Driver's license (for drivers)
    • Government ID (for passengers)
    • Selfie holding ID (identity verification)
  • All documents stored encrypted in AWS S3, linked to the customer's GHL record — accessible by your team with one click
  • Completion tracking: if waiver or documents are not submitted within 24 hours, automated reminders sent via SMS and email
  • Pre-experience confirmation sequence: 48-hour and 2-hour reminders with location, what to wear, what to expect
Deliverables: Digital waiver system · Secure document upload portal · AWS S3 document vault · Automated reminder sequences
PHASE 3 Sales Tracking, Post-Experience Follow-up & Retention Weeks 7–9

What gets built:

  • Sales tracking dashboard: GHL reporting filtered by team member, product type (car + experience), week, month, year — importable historical data via Airtable migration
  • Team attribution: each booking tagged to the team member who worked the lead; leaderboard view available in GHL
  • Post-experience sequence (triggers day of experience):
    • Day 0: "Thank you" message + photo/video delivery link (Google Drive or Dropbox folder auto-shared)
    • Day 1: Review request (Google + Yelp) — personalized with their car and experience
    • Day 3: Referral offer — "Give $50, Get $50" with unique referral link
    • Day 7: Early access offer for next booking
  • Retention automation:
    • Anniversary email on the exact date each year — includes their photo and a return offer
    • Birthday email with a VIP discount code (pulled from GHL contact DOB field)
    • Seasonal campaigns: holidays, car show season, Valentine's Day packages
Deliverables: Sales dashboard · Team tracking · Post-experience automation · Anniversary + birthday retention engine
PHASE 4 AI Voice Agent & Content Automation Weeks 10–13

What gets built:

  • AI Voice Agent (Vapi + ElevenLabs):
    • Answers inbound calls 24/7 in English, Spanish, and one additional language (Mandarin or Portuguese — your choice)
    • Handles: FAQs, pricing, experience descriptions, availability check, booking handoff
    • Function calling: can look up real-time calendar availability, create GHL contact/lead record mid-call
    • Escalation path: if the caller wants a human, transfers to your team's number or logs a callback request
    • All calls recorded and transcribed; summaries logged in GHL contact record
  • AI Content Support:
    • Make automation: when you upload customer photos/videos to a shared folder, AI generates 5 caption variations per platform (TikTok hook, Instagram caption, LinkedIn post, Facebook story, YouTube Short description)
    • Weekly content calendar output: AI generates a 7-day posting plan based on upcoming events, car availability, and seasonal themes
    • Hashtag research and trend-aware copy using OpenAI with browsing
  • Testing, QA, and handover: Full system walk-through with your team, SOPs written, admin training session
Deliverables: Live multilingual AI voice agent · Content generation pipeline · Team training · Full system SOPs

❓ Questions Before Starting

Do you currently use any CRM, booking, or automation tool (GHL, HubSpot, Calendly, etc.)? If so, we need to decide whether to migrate or integrate with it.

What is your current booking and payment process? Do customers pay a deposit or full amount upfront? Is there a specific booking platform you are tied to?

How many team members will need access to the CRM and sales dashboard? Do you want different permission levels (sales rep vs. manager vs. admin)?

Do you have an existing customer database (even a spreadsheet) of past customers we should import for the retention and anniversary campaigns?
